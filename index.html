About Us

Daily Activities

Coloring Children will express themselves by coloring images. They will use crayons, colored pencils, and markers.
Drawing Childrens will be taught how to draw simple images by putting shapes togerther. They will be provided with all the materials they need
Painting As messy as it is can be, children will sometimes paint with their hands and feet. No worries because we will make sure they will go home clean! They will also use washable painting, paint brush and canvas.
